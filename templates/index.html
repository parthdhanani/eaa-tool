Will your course pass an accessibility audit?

Drop a SCORM package. You get a WCAG 2.2 AA report, the exact criteria at risk, a plain-language fix for each, and an honest estimate of the work to compliance. The European Accessibility Act has been in force since June 2025.

Nothing is stored. Your package is analysed in memory and deleted the moment the report is built. This is static analysis, the cheap 80 percent. A signoff still needs a human audit and assistive-tech testing.

Criteria at risk

Mapped to WCAG 2.2 success criteria. Levels A and AA are the bar EN 301 549 sets for the EU Accessibility Act. Static checks cannot judge colour contrast in images, caption accuracy, or whether alt text is actually meaningful, so treat a clean result as a strong floor, not a certificate.
